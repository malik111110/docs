## How It Works

Quick KYC processes document submissions through a secure, fully local pipeline — from image quality checks to OCR extraction to risk evaluation — and surfaces results in a compliance dashboard or via webhook.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload a document">
    Submit a document scan (front, back, and optional selfie) via the dashboard or API. Quick KYC checks image quality before processing begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automated OCR & fraud analysis">
    The system runs multi-pass OCR to extract fields with confidence scores, and performs forensic checks for tampering, expiration, and recapture spoofing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review in the dashboard">
    Compliance officers see a risk score, extracted data, and any flagged signals. They can approve, reject, or request a manual audit directly from the UI.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive results via webhook">
    Optionally supply a webhook URL at submission time to receive the full verification result as soon as processing completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>
