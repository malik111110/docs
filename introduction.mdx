Quick KYC is an on-premises identity verification platform built for compliance teams and developers who cannot afford to send sensitive document data to third-party cloud services. Every stage of the verification pipeline — OCR extraction, fraud detection, image quality analysis, and AI-generated explanations — runs entirely within your own infrastructure. You stay in control of your data, your audit trail, and your compliance posture.

## Who Quick KYC Is For

Quick KYC is designed for two primary audiences who work together in the same verification workflow.

**Compliance officers** use the dashboard to review documents that the automated pipeline has flagged as `UNDER_REVIEW`. You can inspect every extracted field, read the AI-generated explanation of the risk signals, and then approve or reject the record with annotated notes. Every decision you make is recorded in the audit log with a timestamp and your name.

**Developers** integrate Quick KYC into existing onboarding or back-office workflows using the REST API or the Python SDK. You submit documents programmatically, receive a verification ID immediately, and retrieve results by polling or by supplying a webhook URL at submission time.

## Verification Statuses

Every verification record moves through a defined set of statuses during its lifecycle.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `PENDING` | The document has been received and is queued for background processing. |
| `UNDER_REVIEW` | Automated processing is complete. One or more risk signals require a human compliance decision. |
| `APPROVED` | The document has passed automated checks and/or been approved by a compliance officer. |
| `REJECTED` | The document has been rejected — either automatically or by a compliance officer override. |

<Note>
  Status transitions are logged in the audit trail. You can retrieve the full history for any verification record, including the actor, timestamp, and any notes attached to each status change.
</Note>

## Privacy and Data Sovereignty

Quick KYC makes no outbound network requests during document processing. OCR, fraud detection, image quality analysis, and AI explanation generation all run on your own hardware using locally hosted models and algorithms. You can operate Quick KYC in a fully air-gapped environment with no internet access required after installation.
